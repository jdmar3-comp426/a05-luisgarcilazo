A part of Japanese Folkore

Japanese folklore includes many mythical creatures that may seem out of this world, with some of them reassembling humans and animals. This mythical creatures are collectively under the Yokai name. The Bakekujira is one of this, and similar to many Yokai, seems to be a harbinger of conflict to the human race. It is no surprise as Yokai is often translated to specter or demon, terms often associated with evil.
